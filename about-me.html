Welcome!

Nico Martinez Nocito (they/them) is a Rhysling-nominated queer, trans writer of fiction and poetry, often with a speculative bent. Their work has been published by Strange Horizons, Utopia Science Fiction, and Flame Tree Press, as well as numerous anthologies. They spend their free time running, sharing books they love on their blog Rapunzel Reads, and acting in local theater productions.

Learn more about Nico and their writing on Bluesky and Instagram.
