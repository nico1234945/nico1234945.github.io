Awards Eligibility 2025

I've officially gathered my awards-eligible poetry and fiction for 2025! My work is eligible for speculative awards including the Rhysling (poetry), Nebula (fiction and poetry), Hugo, World Fantasy, Bram Stoker, British Fantasy, BSFA, and Dragon awards, as well as others not listed here!

I'd be particularly thrilled if you considered my poem "To Be the Change" (Strange Horizons), which dissects belonging and multigenerational prophecies, and my dark fantasy flash "A Single Song" (Factor Four Magazine), a dark reimagining of the Grimm tale 'The Singing Bone.' Both are free to read online. I'm also in my first year of eligibility for the Astounding Award.

Check out my full eligibility post here!

If you're interested in reading one of my pieces for awards consideration that isn't available for free online, feel free to reach out to me via the form on my Contact page.

2026 Rhysling Nominees!

I'm thrilled to announce that my poems "To Be the Change" and "Eagle-Eyed" are finalists for the 2026 Rhysling Award in the short-form category!
